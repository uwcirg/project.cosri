Prescribing opioids correctly requires close attention to clinical guidelines and Washington state rules in order to maximize patient benefit and minimize harm. Our team at the University of Washington has developed and implemented a new tool that health organizations and practices can use to access the Washington State PDMP database. The COSRI system can either be integrated into the Electronic Health Record (EHR) or run as a freestanding application. COSRI provides easy access to patient prescription histories in the PDMP as well as integrated clinical decision support with Centers for Disease Control guidelines and Washington state rules.

We currently have two test sites where COSRI has been implemented. We are working closely with these sites to provide continuous, iterative system development based on user experience and to conduct short- and longer-term evaluation of COSRI.

Responsive, effective clinical application development requires a unique set of engagement and resources. The COSRI timeline below both reflects our experiences and planning and serves as an example of the commitment required for the development successful electronic clinical support tools.
