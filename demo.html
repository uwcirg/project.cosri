About Project Timeline Demo

COSRI is actively being adapted and updated with regular on-going releases based on user feedback and activity monitoring. This Demo instance reflects our current test production systems in operation at our two WA state clinical sites. Updates and new releases will occur consistently with updates and releases of our active site instances.

The patient examples in our Demo system includes real queries and pathways for the WA State Prescription Drug Monitoring Program (PMP) server through the Washington Health Information Exchange, but the patients and data presented are not real. While patients and their data have been created to appear realistic to provide a good example of patients who may be receiving controlled substances, their information has been entirely fabricated by the COSRI Team. Any resemblance to real living persons is by coincidence.

We use a UW-customized, UW-hosted version of the open-source “SMART App Launcher” to run the demo system outside an Electronic Health Record (EHR)
